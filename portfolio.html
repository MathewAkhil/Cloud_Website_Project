Vertech:


Vertech is a project that I created with a couple of friends at the TAMU Hack hackathon. This program generates a random shape and instructs the user to find the area of the shape. The program finds the area by dividing the shape into smaller ones, finding the area of those small shapes, and adding them all up together to get the total area.
